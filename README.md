## About this Project

In this project, I analyzed the relationship between Bitcoin market sentiment and trader performance.

I used two datasets:
- Historical trading data from Hyperliquid
- Bitcoin Fear & Greed Index

The main goal was to understand how market sentiment (Fear, Greed, Extreme Fear, and Extreme Greed) affects trader performance and trading behavior.

- Win rate comparison

- Long vs Short analysis
- Profit by coin
- Hour-wise trading analysis
- Day-wise trading analysis
- Correlation heatmap
- Top profitable traders

---

## Key Findings

- Traders earned higher average profits during Extreme Greed.
- Fear had the highest trading activity.
- The win rate was better during Greed than Fear.
- Larger trade sizes resulted in higher trading fees.
- Market sentiment had a clear impact on trader behavior and profitability.
